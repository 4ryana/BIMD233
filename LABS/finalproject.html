Final Project Proposal:

The Lash Code by Aryana Villafuerte

As my final project I would like to design an interactive website about eyelash extensions called Lash Code.
The content will consist of information about lash styles, sizes, and textures. The website will also include style
recommendations for different eye shapes and sizes. For a color theme, I would like to use a neutral color palette
but also incorporate different hues of pink. For alignment I will be implementing the CRAP design rules and utilize
a lot of organization and structure for the page. I will have internal links for each category of 'style' 'length'
and 'texture' that will provide a deep understanding of each lash element. For font I will be utilizing a monospaced
font to carry on the theme of lash coding.

I want to implement a behavior portion to the site by allowing visitors to complete a survey or form of what style of
lashes they would like and also provide information about their eye shapes and size as well as any concerns they
may have. For repository organization I will have an images directory that contains examples of lash styles, eye
shapes, and a website header or logo. I will also have a video directory that contains content of clients modeling
lash styles.
